GLOBAL WARMING

Global warming is the long-term heating of Earth's surface observed since the pre-industrial period (between 1850 and 1900)
due to human activities,primarily fossil fuel burning which increases heat-trapping Greenhouse gas levels in Earth's atmosphere.
This term is not interchangeable with the term "climate change".

Major reasons for Global Warming

  • Deforestation
  • Oil Drilling
  • Power plants
  • Industrialization
  • Transport and Vehicles

AFGHANISTAN

The country that got more affected by Global Warming

Afghanistan. Between 1950 and 2020, the temperatures in Afghanistan increased by 1.8ºC, and an optimistic view of the climate crisis in the country still shows a minimum increase of 1.4°C by 2050 (the worst case scenario would see a 6ºC increase by the end of this century)

Preventaion methods for Global Warming

Afforestation

A single tree will absorb one ton of carbon dioxide over its lifetime.

Recycle

We can save 2,400 pounds of carbon dioxide per year by recycling just half of your household waste.

Turn off appliances

Simply turning off your television, DVD player, and computer, when you're not using them, will save you thousands of pounds of carbon dioxide a year.

Usage of Solar panels

Using the power of the sun rather than electricity allows us to harness energy rather than waste energy.

Public Transportation

Usage of Public Transporation leads to decrease of carbon monoxide[CO] which are emitted by Vechiles.
